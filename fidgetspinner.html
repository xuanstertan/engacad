Fidget Spinner :

1) Create a new sketch and select the XY-plane as the sketch plane

2) Draw a diameter 84 circle at the origin and change it to construction line

3) Draw a diameter 22 and 27 cicle at the origin too

4) Draw a 29mm line in the Y-axis and change it to construction line

5) Draw a diameter 26 circle at the tip of the line

6) Go to "Create" tab and select polygon > circumscribe polygon. Afterwards, change the dimension to 13mm

Go to "Create" tab and select circular pattern
-Select the parts which you want to circular patter (circle + polygon)
-centre point is the origin
-angular spaciing is "angle"
- Quantity is "3"

1) Go to "Create" tab and select arc > 3 point arc.

2) Once done, select tangent constraint

Go to "Create" tab and select cicular constaint once again
-Select the (arc)
-centre point is the origin
-Quantity is "3"

1) Once done, clcik on the "Finish Sketch"

2) Extrude
-Direction is "Symmetric"
-Distance is "5mm"

1) After extruding, go to the "Modify" tab and select fillet

2) Click on both the top and bottom face of the spinner and fillet is by "5mm"

3) Click "OK" and we are DONE modelling the fidget spinner
